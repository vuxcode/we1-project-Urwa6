Calligraphy

Calligraphy is a visual art related to writing. Calligraphy means beautiful writing and comes
from the Greek “καλλιγραφία” words “beauty” (Kallos) and “to write” (Graphein).
And while the meaning of the word is “beautiful writing”. I think we can agree that Calligraphy
is much more than just a way to write words in a beautiful way.

Fashion Illustration

Fashion Illustration is the art of communicating fashion ideas in a visual form that originates with illustration, drawing and painting and also known as Fashion sketching. It is mainly used by fashion designers to brainstorm their ideas on paper or digitally.
